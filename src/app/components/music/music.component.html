Djerba’s Beat, Your Heart’s Pulse

The music at RE:SET is a journey – one that mirrors the island’s energy, from the sun-soaked afternoons to the starry, rhythmic nights. Our soundscape weaves together groovy house beats, deep, hypnotic techno, and soulful minimal rhythms, all designed to carry you deeper into the moment.

By day, let the ambient waves and local sounds invite you to explore Djerba’s vibrant culture. By night, lose yourself in the energy of underground icons and visionary artists, each performance a celebration of creativity and connection.

It’s an invitation to breathe with the island, to feel its pulse, and to let the music guide your journey.
